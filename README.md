# Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

## Project Overview

I built this project to explore which factors are most strongly associated with airline passenger satisfaction.

Using passenger survey data, I analyzed how service quality, flight delays, travel class, and customer loyalty relate to overall satisfaction. The project combines Python for data analysis, SQL for business queries, and Tableau for dashboard development.

The goal was not only to identify patterns in the data, but also to translate those findings into practical business recommendations.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information about airline passengers, including:

- Customer demographics
- Travel characteristics
- Flight distance
- Service ratings
- Flight delays
- Customer satisfaction outcomes

The target variable is **Satisfaction**, which is categorized as:

- Satisfied
- Neutral or Dissatisfied

## Key Findings

### Overall Satisfaction

Only **43.33%** of passengers reported being satisfied with their travel experience.

### Service Quality Matters

The largest differences between satisfied and dissatisfied passengers were observed in:

- Online Boarding
- Inflight Entertainment
- Seat Comfort
- Cleanliness

These service-related attributes showed a stronger relationship with satisfaction than operational factors such as delays.

### Travel Class Has a Strong Impact

Business Class passengers reported significantly higher satisfaction levels than Economy passengers.

| Travel Class | Satisfaction Rate |
|-------------|------------------|
| Business | 69.43% |
| Eco Plus | 24.61% |
| Economy | 18.61% |

### Loyal Customers Are More Satisfied

Customer loyalty was strongly associated with satisfaction.

| Customer Type | Satisfaction Rate |
|--------------|------------------|
| Loyal Customer | 47.73% |
| Disloyal Customer | 23.67% |

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, several opportunities for improvement were identified:

- Improve the digital boarding experience.
- Continue investing in onboard comfort and entertainment.
- Reduce operational delays where possible.
- Improve the Economy Class customer experience.
- Strengthen customer loyalty and retention programs.
